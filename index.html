ABOUT

Every year since 1938, the organization presents Stern Grove Festival, an admission-free performing arts series, in Sigmund Stern Grove, a beautiful outdoor amphitheater located at 19th Avenue and Sloat Boulevard in San Francisco.

Stern Grove Festival Association is a non-profit cultural organization committed to providing the people of the Bay Area with admission-free access to diverse performing arts.

THE BIG PICNIC

The Big Picnic, an afternoon of music, food, drinks, and dancing in beautiful Sigmund Stern Grove.The Big Picnic is a fundraising event benefiting Stern Grove Festival and its mission to present admission-free concerts and outreach programs to the people of the Bay Area.

Please note: The Big Picnic Concert on June 22, 2014 is admission-free and open to the public, but seating is first-come, first-served. Purchasing a ticket for The Big Picnic Party will guarantee you a reserved seat at the concert
